Who We Are

At Mindease, our mission is to help people take control of their mental health and wellbeing. We believe that everyone deserves access to tools and resources that can help them manage their emotions, reduce stress, and improve their overall quality of life. That's why we've created an easy-to-use platform that allows users to track their mood, write in their journal, and answer positive checkup questions that can help them identify areas where they might need additional support.
Our team is made up of experienced mental health professionals and software developers who are passionate about making a difference in people's lives. We understand that seeking help for mental health can be daunting, which is why we've designed our platform to be intuitive and user-friendly. Whether you're looking to manage anxiety, depression, or simply improve your overall wellbeing, Mindease is here to support you every step of the way.
